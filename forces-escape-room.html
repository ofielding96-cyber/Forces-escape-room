🔬 Key Forces Facts — Revision Summary
• Force = mass × acceleration (F = ma)
• Weight = mass × gravitational field strength (W = mg)
• Newton's 1st Law: balanced forces → constant velocity
• Newton's 3rd Law: every action has equal & opposite reaction
• Pressure = Force ÷ Area (P = F/A)
• Moment = Force × perpendicular distance (M = Fd)
• Resultant force = vector sum of all forces
• g on Earth ≈ 10 N/kg (or 9.8 N/kg)
• Friction always opposes motion
• Balanced moments → object in equilibrium (principle of moments)
